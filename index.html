Who Is Damson Idris

Damson Idris is a British actor of Nigerian descent, born on September 1, 1991, in London, United Kingdom. He grew up in South London as one of six children and discovered his passion for acting in his teenage years. After studying drama at Brunel University, he trained further at a London drama academy, sharpening his skills. Damson began his acting career in 2012 and rose to international fame with his lead role in the hit series Snowfall, where he portrayed Franklin Saint. His performances are known for their emotional depth and commanding screen presence, making him one of Hollywood’s most promising young talents.
